About Me

I turn a love of plants into places people love.

I'm Kale — an entrepreneur from the small town of Ulen, Minnesota, now planted in Fargo, North Dakota. I studied business administration at North Dakota State University, and before I'd even graduated, I opened my first shop: Botanical Brothers, a houseplant store built on the simple idea that everyone's home deserves a little more green.

Four years of running a plant shop taught me what no classroom could — how to build a brand, a community, and a business from the ground up. Now I'm taking everything I've learned and going bigger: Plant Perfect Fargo, a full garden center opening in 2027.
